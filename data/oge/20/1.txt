Sochi is often called the 'Summer Capital' of Russia. It is a popular summer seaside resort. The city stretches for 145 kilometres along the shore of the Black Sea. Each year a lot of tourists come to the city to enjoy its beaches, green parks and gardens. Sochi is famous for its climate. It has warm summers and mild winters. It is a good place for playing sports. The mountains near the city attract skiers from all over the world. In 2014, the Winter Olympic Games were held in Sochi. It was the first time for Winter Olympic Games to take place in a subtropical climate.
