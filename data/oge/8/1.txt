Artek is a famous international children's camp in Russia. It was founded in 1925, on the Black Sea. Artek was named after the local river. The camp was planned as a summer holiday place for children from all over the country. At first, they stayed in tents. Nowadays, the camp has got about 400 modern buildings. There are sports grounds, tennis courts, swimming pools and a modern concert hall in the camp. Artek works all year round. It offers programs for recreation, education and sports development. A lot of children have met true friends there. To keep warm memories about Artek, everybody should take a piece of coal from the farewell fire and bring it home.
